Ma Cuisine takes take-away food into the realms of gourmet dining

Prepared and devised by Michelin-trained chef ChristopheLacroix it brings to Bath a unique concept: food of exquisite quality delivered to your door at prices barely more than fish and chips.

Savour the flavour of “salmon au vin” with roasted fennel; of scallops and shittake mushrooms in spaghetti with a lobster sauce (as a shared starter or main); of classic “coq au vin”; of mushroom and blue cheese risotto…

These are flavours of distinction. Each dish prepared with the same care and attention to the delicacies of taste as Christophe was trained to deliver to customers in some of the finest restaurants of Europe – and in Paris, Lausanne, Monaco and St Lucia.

Using fresh locally sourced produce, the dishes vary with the seasons – seafood prepared according to the best catches of the day, vegetarian options with the pickings of the morning dew – all providing a freshness of flavours that delights.

Choose from our menu board or call and discuss your special needs. Whether that is for a one-off special occasion or to devise special dishes to account for preferences, or allergies.

We welcome your calls because Ma Cuisine is passionate about delivering to you the food you love.

Thank you and Bon Appétit!
